JellyPop!

About

JellyPop! is an action-puzzle game inspired by games like Tetris and Bejeweled. Use your finger to connect chains of jellies to get higher scores. Once the screen is filled up the game ends.
This game is now available on Google Play and will be coming soon to the App Store.
